Welcome to SeaQuel!

SeaQuel is the ultimate social media app for cruise ship enthusiasts. Connect with fellow cruisers, share your experiences, and stay updated on the latest news and events in the world of cruising.

Features:

  • Find and connect with other cruise enthusiasts
  • Share photos and videos from your cruise adventures
  • Discover new cruise destinations and itineraries
  • Get insider tips and recommendations from experienced cruisers
  • Show off your cruise ducks and find SeaQuel ducks to unlock special prizes in the app
