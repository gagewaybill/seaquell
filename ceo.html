Meet Our CEO: Gage Waybill

Welcome to SeaQuel, where innovation meets passion for the seas! Meet our CEO, Gage Waybill, the visionary behind our cruise ship social media app.

About Gage Waybill:

Gage Waybill, a maritime enthusiast and tech entrepreneur, is the driving force behind SeaQuel. With a deep-rooted love for cruising and a keen eye for technological advancement, Gage embarked on a journey to revolutionize the cruise industry.

Inspiration:

In his own words, Gage Waybill shares what inspired him to create SeaQuel. "As a lifelong lover of the ocean and a frequent cruiser myself, I realized there was a gap in the market for a dedicated social platform tailored to cruise enthusiasts like me. The idea of connecting cruisers from around the globe, sharing experiences, and fostering a community where everyone feels at home was what fueled my passion to create SeaQuel. I envisioned a space where fellow cruisers could come together, exchange stories, discover new destinations, and forge friendships that transcend the boundaries of the sea."

Vision:

Gage Waybill's vision for SeaQuel extends beyond just a social media platform; it's about building a vibrant community of cruise enthusiasts united by their love for the ocean. With SeaQuel, Gage aims to redefine the cruise experience, making it more interactive, engaging, and memorable for everyone on board.

Join us on this extraordinary voyage with Gage Waybill at the helm, as we embark on a journey to connect cruisers worldwide through SeaQuel.

Meet Our Co-founders: Avrien Ayers and AJ Carson

Avrien Ayers and AJ Carson, both seasoned entrepreneurs and avid cruisers, joined forces with Gage Waybill to bring SeaQuel to life. With their combined expertise in business development, marketing, and technology, Avrien and AJ are instrumental in shaping the vision and direction of SeaQuel.

Together, they share Gage's passion for creating a vibrant community of cruise enthusiasts and are dedicated to making SeaQuel the go-to platform for cruisers worldwide.
